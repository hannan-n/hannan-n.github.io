AUD/USD Covered Interest Parity (CIP) Model

This project analyses whether AUD/USD exchange-rate movements follow the Covered Interest Parity condition. Using 10 years of monthly FRED, OECD, and Yahoo Finance data, I computed theoretical forward rates and compared them to actual forward prices to identify basis spreads and market frictions.

The model highlights when CIP holds tightly and when liquidity divergences or funding pressures cause deviations. This type of analysis is used in FX strategy and macro-trading teams to evaluate arbitrage boundaries.
