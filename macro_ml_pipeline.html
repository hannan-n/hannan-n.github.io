Macroeconomic & Data Science Pipeline

A complete macro-forecasting workflow that integrates economic time-series modelling with machine learning. The pipeline covers unit-root testing, lag creation, feature engineering, ARIMA forecasting, OLS estimation, and gradient-boosting (XGBoost) modelling.

This project shows how economists and data scientists combine theoretical methods with ML flexibility to build robust forecasting systems used in policy analysis and investment research.
