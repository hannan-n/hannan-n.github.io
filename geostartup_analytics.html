GeoStartup Analytics

This geospatial analysis uses Folium mapping, clustering, and geo-visualization to explore global startup activity. It highlights innovation hotspots by combining funding data with geographic coordinates and K-Means clustering.

The project demonstrates how analysts use spatial data to understand regional entrepreneurship patterns and ecosystem strengths.
